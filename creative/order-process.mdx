Every order at Nocturne Designs follows the same process. Following these steps ensures customers receive consistent, quality service and your work is properly logged.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Customer Opens a Ticket">
    When a user opens an order ticket, you may use the `.format` command to send the order format. **Do not claim the ticket immediately** — wait until you are ready to take the order.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customer Fills Out the Format">
    Make sure the customer completes the order format in full. This gives you everything you need to meet their expectations. Do not proceed until the format is filled out.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Collect Payment Before Starting">
    Always require payment **before** you begin working on the design. Use the official Nocturne Designs verified payment link — never your personal link.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete the Design">
    Work on the order based on the customer's format. Use Nocturne Designs watermarks on any previews or work-in-progress images you share.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run Quality Control">
    Before delivering the final design, run it through the QC service. No design goes to a customer without passing QC.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deliver and Log">
    Deliver the completed design to the customer via DM. Once the customer is satisfied, submit your order log. **Order logs are submitted after the customer confirms satisfaction — not at the point of claiming or payment.**
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
Do not use DMs for anything other than final delivery. All ordering, communication, and payment must happen in the ticket channel.
</Warning>

## Key Rules at a Glance

| Step | Rule |
|---|---|
| Ticket opens | Use `.format`, do not claim immediately |
| Before starting | Require payment first |
| During work | Use Nocturne Designs watermarks on previews |
| Before delivery | Pass QC check |
| After delivery | Submit order log only when customer is satisfied |
