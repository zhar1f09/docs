As a Creative Team member at Nocturne Designs, you earn a **95% commission rate** on every order you complete. Here's how the payment process works.

## Commission Rate

You receive 95% of the order value for every completed design. The remaining amount covers company overhead.

## When You Get Paid

<Info>
Payment can take **up to one month** from order completion. This delay is due to the time Robux takes to process through the platform.
</Info>

Payment timelines are outside Nocturne Designs' direct control — Roblox's Robux processing times determine when funds arrive.

## Order Logs

Your order log is what triggers the payment process. Follow these rules:

- **Submit your order log only after the customer confirms they are satisfied** with the final design.
- Do not submit logs at the time of claiming the order or receiving payment from the customer.
- Incomplete or incorrectly submitted logs may delay your payment.

<Warning>
Submitting an order log before the customer is satisfied is a policy violation and may affect your payout.
</Warning>

## Questions and Concerns

An HR representative is always available to help with any payment questions or concerns. If you believe there is an error with your pay, reach out to HR with your order log details.
