Quality control (QC) is a mandatory step for every order at Nocturne Designs. It exists to ensure every design meets the company's standards before it reaches the customer.

<Warning>
You must always use the QC service on all designs before delivering to the customer. Skipping QC is a policy violation.
</Warning>

## What QC Checks

The QC process reviews your design for:

- Overall quality and polish
- Accuracy against the customer's format requirements
- Correct use of Nocturne Designs watermarks on preview images
- Any issues that would require a revision before delivery

## Watermarks

When sharing any preview or work-in-progress version of an order — whether in the ticket or elsewhere — you **must** use official Nocturne Designs watermarks. Watermarks are removed only on the final delivery.

<Note>
If you are unsure how to access or apply watermarks, ask an HR representative.
</Note>

## Submitting for QC

Submit your completed design through the QC service before reaching out to the customer for final delivery. Only after your design passes QC should you proceed to delivery.

<Tip>
If your design comes back with QC feedback, address all notes before resubmitting. Do not deliver a design that has not passed.
</Tip>
