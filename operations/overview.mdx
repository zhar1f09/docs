The Operations team at Nocturne Designs keeps the day-to-day running of the server and support system on track. Whether you are handling customer support tickets or managing escalations, your work ensures customers get the help they need.

<Info>
Operations (Community Support) is a **voluntary position** — you are not compensated financially for this role. Your contribution helps the community and the company.
</Info>
