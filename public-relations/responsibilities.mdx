As a member of the Public Relations team at Nocturne Designs, you are responsible for managing the company's image and maintaining positive relationships with the community.

## Core Responsibilities

<Steps>
  <Step title="Community Engagement">
    Monitor and engage with the Nocturne Designs community in server channels. Respond to questions, acknowledge feedback, and keep interactions positive and professional.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Announcements and Updates">
    Draft and post official announcements on behalf of Nocturne Designs. All announcements must be reviewed and approved before publishing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reputation Management">
    Address community concerns in a timely and professional manner. If a situation escalates, escalate it to HR rather than handling it unilaterally.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Partnerships and Outreach">
    Support any official outreach efforts or partnership discussions the company undertakes. Do not make commitments on behalf of Nocturne Designs without prior approval.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What Is Not Your Role

- **Order handling** — direct customers with order questions to the Creative Team or a support ticket.
- **Policy enforcement** — refer rule violations to HR.
- **Payment disputes** — route these to HR immediately.

<Tip>
When in doubt about whether something falls under your role, check with HR before acting.
</Tip>
