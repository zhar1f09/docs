Everything the PR team says publicly represents Nocturne Designs. These guidelines ensure that all communications are consistent, professional, and aligned with the company's values.

## Tone and Voice

- **Be professional and approachable.** Avoid overly casual language in official communications.
- **Be clear and concise.** Say what you mean without unnecessary filler.
- **Stay positive.** Even when addressing criticism, keep the tone constructive.

## Approval Process

<Warning>
No official announcement or statement should be published without prior review and approval. Unauthorized statements can cause serious reputational harm.
</Warning>

Before posting any announcement or official response:

1. Draft the content and share it internally for review.
2. Get approval from the appropriate lead or HR representative.
3. Publish only after approval is confirmed.

## What to Avoid

- Making promises or commitments without authorization.
- Sharing unconfirmed information or rumors.
- Engaging in arguments or debates with community members.
- Speaking on topics outside your role (orders, payments, policy enforcement).

<Note>
If you receive a question or complaint you are not equipped to answer, direct the person to the appropriate channel and notify HR if needed.
</Note>
