Professionalism at Nocturne Designs means behaving in a way that reflects your role and upholds the reputation of the company in every public space.

## Acting Your Rank

All employees must act in accordance with their position when in any public area of the server. This means:

- **Representing your department appropriately** — how you present yourself reflects your team.
- **Avoiding behavior that undermines your role** — do not act in ways that contradict your responsibilities or rank.
- **Leading by example** — senior staff are especially expected to model the behavior they want to see from newer members.

## In Practice

<Steps>
  <Step title="Know your role">
    Understand the expectations that come with your specific rank and department.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Behave consistently">
    Maintain professional conduct whether you are in a public channel, ticket, or any other visible space.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Represent Nocturne Designs">
    Every interaction is a reflection of the company. Conduct yourself accordingly.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
If you are unsure what behavior is appropriate for your role, speak to an HR representative.
</Note>
