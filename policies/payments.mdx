Nocturne Designs processes all customer payments through a single, verified company link. This ensures orders are tracked correctly and protects both you and the customer.

<Warning>
Never use your own personal payment link. Only the verified Nocturne Designs payment link is permitted for all transactions.
</Warning>

## Rules

- **Always use the company-verified payment link** when collecting payment from a customer.
- **Never share your own Robux link** or direct customers to pay you personally.
- **Do not begin work** on any order until payment has been confirmed through the official link.
- **DMs are not for payments.** Any attempt to collect payment through direct messages is a violation of this policy.

<Note>
If you are unsure which link to use, ask an HR representative before proceeding with any order.
</Note>
