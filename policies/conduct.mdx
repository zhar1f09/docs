How you communicate with customers and coworkers reflects directly on Nocturne Designs. This page covers the conduct rules every staff member must follow at all times.

## Direct Messages (DMs)

DMs have a single permitted use: **delivering the completed design to the customer at the end of an order.**

<Warning>
Using DMs to take orders, request payment, or redirect customers away from official channels is strictly prohibited and will result in disciplinary action.
</Warning>

All ordering, payment, and communication must happen within the proper ticket channels in the server.

## Respect

Every staff member is expected to treat customers and fellow employees with respect at all times.

- Use professional, courteous language in all interactions.
- Do not demean, insult, or speak poorly of customers or coworkers.
- If a situation becomes difficult, de-escalate or reach out to HR — do not engage in arguments.

<Tip>
If you encounter a customer who is rude or unreasonable, notify HR rather than responding in kind.
</Tip>
