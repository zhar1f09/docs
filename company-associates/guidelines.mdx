Nocturne Designs expects every company associate to hold themselves to a high standard. These guidelines apply to everything from how you talk in the server to how you handle unexpected situations.

## Server Behavior

- **Be respectful at all times** — to customers, coworkers, and leadership.
- **Stay on topic** in work channels. Keep casual conversation to the designated areas.
- **Represent the company well** in every public channel.

## Customer Interactions

When engaging with customers:

- Be helpful, professional, and courteous.
- If a customer has an order or payment question, direct them to the appropriate ticket channel.
- Do not make promises, offer discounts, or commit to anything on behalf of the company without approval.

<Warning>
Never attempt to take orders or collect payments outside of the official ticket system. This applies to company associates just as it does to every other role.
</Warning>

## Working with Other Teams

- Respect the workflows and processes of other departments.
- Do not interfere with Creative Team orders, PR communications, or Operations tickets unless specifically asked to help.
- Share feedback with your lead rather than acting unilaterally.

<Note>
For any situation you are unsure about, the right move is always to ask HR before acting.
</Note>
