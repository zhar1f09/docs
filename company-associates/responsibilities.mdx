As a Company Associate at Nocturne Designs, your responsibilities span supporting operations, maintaining a positive server environment, and assisting other departments where needed.

## Core Responsibilities

<Steps>
  <Step title="Maintain an Active Presence">
    Stay active in the server and be available during your designated times. A consistent presence helps the company run smoothly and shows reliability.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Support Other Departments">
    Assist the Creative Team, Public Relations, and Operations teams as needed. Associates serve as a cross-functional resource for the company.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Engage with Customers Professionally">
    If you interact with customers in any capacity, do so professionally and in line with company policies. Direct order or payment questions to the appropriate team.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Follow Escalation Procedures">
    If you encounter issues beyond your role — disputes, rule violations, or urgent problems — escalate them to HR promptly.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Scope of Role

Company Associates are generalists. You are not expected to take on the specialized work of designers or the public-facing work of PR. Your value is in supporting the team and keeping things running.

<Tip>
If you are unsure whether a task falls within your responsibilities, ask your department lead or HR.
</Tip>
