For the complete documentation index, see [llms.txt](https://commander-coast.gitbook.io/staff-guides/llms.txt). This page is also available as [Markdown](https://commander-coast.gitbook.io/staff-guides/policies.md).

All policies listed here must be followed and failure to do so may lead to infractions.

- Payments - All payments must be done with a Coast Customs verified link, do not use you’re own link.
 
- DM’s - Dm’s may only be used to deliver the design at the end, any attempts to lead customers to you’re DM’s to order or pay are not tolerated.
 
- Respect - You must always be respectful to customers and to other employees.
 
- Professionalism - All employees must act their rank when in public spaces of the server.
