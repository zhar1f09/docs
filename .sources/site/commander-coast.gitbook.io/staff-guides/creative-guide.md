You’re role as a designer is to complete orders and serve the customers needs. You will be paid a 95% commission rate per order.

This guide will list all important aspects of the Creative Department.

- When a user opens a ticket you are allowed to use the .format command but cannot claim right then and there.
 
- When taking an order you must always require payment before starting the order.
 
- Make sure the user fills out the format in order to insure you can complete their expectations.
 
- You are required to use Coast Customs watermarks when showing an order.
 
- You must always use our QC service on all designs before serving to the customer.
 
- Order logs will be sent when the design is complete and the customer is satisfied, not when you claim and they pay.
 
- It can take up to a month to be paid due to how long Robux will take to process.
 
- An hr will always be available to adress any questions or concerns.
