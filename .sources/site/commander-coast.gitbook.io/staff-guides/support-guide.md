You‘re job as a Community Support member is to assist with users request in support tickets. You will not be paid as this is a completely voluntary position.

This guide will list all important aspects of the Community Support Team.

- When assisting a user you must always be professional and respectful to the users question or request.
 
- Don’t be afraid to ask for help, if you need to escalate the ticket notify an HR.
